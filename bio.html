Artist Bio

Wenhao is an urban designer and sketch artist from Hunan, China. His love for communities and art began in childhood, sketching busy urban alleyways and vibrant street life. He believes in the power of small, place-based changes to improve quality of life. You'll often find him exploring Bay Area cities with his sketchbook, discovering new foods, and capturing the stories of the built environment.
