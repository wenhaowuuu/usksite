Washington Square Park

2024 San Francisco, CA

Washington Square Park in North Beach serves as a beloved neighborhood commons where locals practice tai chi, walk dogs, and enjoy outdoor activities. The park's mature trees provide shade for families and elderly residents seeking respite. This green heart of Little Italy maintains its community character amid urban density.
