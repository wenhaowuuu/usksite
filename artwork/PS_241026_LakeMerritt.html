Lake Merritt Reflections

2024 Oakland, CA

Oakland's Lake Merritt serves as an urban oasis where joggers, families, and nature enthusiasts converge. The calm waters reflect both the natural landscape and surrounding cityscape in perfect harmony. This beloved local landmark offers respite from city life while remaining at its heart.
