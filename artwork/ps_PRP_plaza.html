PRP Plaza

2024 Bay Area, CA

A bustling public plaza where office workers, residents, and visitors converge during lunch hours and evening commutes. Modern architecture frames this urban gathering space designed for community interaction. The plaza exemplifies contemporary urban planning that prioritizes pedestrian-friendly public spaces.
