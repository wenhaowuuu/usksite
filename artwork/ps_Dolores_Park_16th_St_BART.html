Dolores Park & 16th Street BART

2025 San Francisco, CA

The vibrant intersection where Dolores Park meets the 16th Street BART station captures San Francisco's diverse urban rhythm. Park-goers and commuters share this dynamic space throughout the day. The juxtaposition of recreational green space and transit infrastructure defines modern city living.
