Berkeley Concerts

2024 Berkeley, CA

Musicians gather in a Berkeley park for an impromptu outdoor concert, bringing community together through music. The informal setting allows for intimate performances under California's endless blue sky. Local residents pause their daily routines to enjoy this spontaneous cultural moment.
